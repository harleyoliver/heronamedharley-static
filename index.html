Who Is Harley Oliver?

My name is a Harley Oliver, and I am a Geelong based Web Developer, Motorbike enthusiast and MMA connoisseur.

I was born in the tiny mallee town of Manangatang; and moved to Geelong when I was 5. In 2011, I finished a Diploma of IT.

Over the next 9 years, I would work at a variety of agencies - make mistakes, learn new skills, constantly improve. I've spoken at conferences, organised community meetups and helped other devs along the way.

In the last decade, I have come to fully understand the power of a websites, the importance of storytelling and the nuance of audience.

And now, I want to help you do the same.
